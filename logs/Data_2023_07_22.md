# Descriptions
1. This is the second test of imaging the AAV1 packed iAChSnFR/GACh3.0 mixed with AAV1 packed cre-dependent (ChAT-cre) mCherry, respectively. 
# Findings
1. The expressions of GACh and iAChSnFR are both find, but the mCherry is relatively weak but visible.
2. The sampling rate of imaging acquisition need to be increased to see more dynamics (spikes).
    - Due to the low sampling rate (10 Hz) and strong bleaching, the dynamics of ACh are generally not prominent either with GACh or iAChSnFR, check example below. 
        - 2024_07_22-0006 (Slice 3L, GACh3.0)
        - 2024_07_22-0010 (Slice 3R, iAChSnFR)
3. The bleaching trends in imaging data needs to be calibrated.
4. This time the GACh expression is stronger than iAChSnFR. The difference compared to previous results acquired on 2023-06-13. The difference may resulted from unfamiliar/unstable injection.
